About

Innoviyal is the official association of the Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning (AIML) department at MCET. Our association stands as a dynamic platform for students to explore and excel in the rapidly evolving world of AI and ML.

With a focus on fostering intelligence, driving innovation, and making a lasting impact, Innoviyal is committed to empowering students to push boundaries, solve real-world problems, and collaborate on cutting-edge technologies.
