Problem Statements

Choose one of the following problem statements for your hackathon project:

1. Smart College Bus Transportation System with Live Tracking

Develop a smart transportation management system that allows students and staff to track college buses in real time using GPS technology. The system should provide accurate arrival predictions, route optimization, and safety alerts to reduce waiting time, improve transportation efficiency, and ensure a secure commute experience.

2. College Tour in Metaverse

Create a virtual metaverse experience that allows users to explore the college campus from anywhere in the world. Through 3D environments, interactive avatars, and immersive VR/AR technology, prospective students and visitors can engage with campus facilities, labs, and events virtually, offering a futuristic digital tour experience.

3. Prediction of Energy Consumption Using Machine Learning Techniques

Build a machine learning-based predictive model to analyze and forecast the college's energy consumption patterns. Using historical data, weather conditions, and usage trends, the system should provide accurate energy demand predictions, helping institutions reduce costs, enhance sustainability, and optimize power usage.

4. Virtual Lab Assistant Using Voice Commands

Introduce a virtual AI-powered lab assistant that responds to voice commands for performing tasks such as equipment control, data logging, and experiment guidance. This innovation should enhance lab safety, improve accessibility, and assist students in conducting experiments hands-free with real-time support.

5. Solar Panel Cleaning Mechanism

Design an automated solar panel cleaning system that enhances the efficiency of solar power generation. Using smart sensors and mechanical brushes or air jets, the system should detect dust accumulation and perform scheduled cleaning, ensuring maximum energy output and reduced maintenance costs.

6. Smart Sanitary Napkin Vending Machines for Hostel Use

Develop a smart IoT-enabled vending machine for sanitary napkins within hostels. It should enable cashless payment options, real-time stock monitoring, and automatic refill alerts. The system promotes hygiene, convenience, and privacy while addressing a vital women's health need in educational institutions.

7. Laser-Followed Seating Mechanism in Meeting Halls

Introduce an automated seating management system using laser tracking technology to guide attendees to available seats in meeting halls. The system should use sensors and laser pointers to direct individuals efficiently, reducing crowding and confusion while enhancing time management and user experience.

8. Staff Live Location Sharing Inside College Campus

Develop a campus-based live location tracking system for staff members using indoor navigation and Bluetooth/GPS technology. The system should allow authorized users to view staff locations in real time, improving coordination, emergency response, and administrative communication within the college premises.

Rules & Regulations

  • First come, first served - Maximum 30 teams allowed

  • Internet access will be provided during the event

Judging Criteria

  • Innovation & Creativity: Unique approach to solving the problem
  • Technical Implementation: Quality of code and prototype functionality
  • Feasibility: Practicality and real-world applicability of the solution
  • Presentation: Clear explanation and communication skills
  • Impact: Potential to solve the given problem effectively
